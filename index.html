o_O by weepy

HTML binding for Lulz

  • Power of KnockoutJS, with the agility of Backbone

  • Elegantly binds objects to HTML

  • Proxies through jQuery (or whatever $ is)

  • Automatic dependency resolution

  • Plays well with others
